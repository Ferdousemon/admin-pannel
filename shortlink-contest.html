The 1xpepe Referral (Affiliate) Program Consists Of More Than 1 Level. What Does This Mean?


If A Person Uses Your Referral Link To Register, He Becomes Your Referral, And You Will Receive 15% Of Every Earnings He Makes. This Is A First-Level Or Tier 1 Referral.


If This Same Person Invites Someone Else To The Service Using His Own Referral Link, Then He Gets A First-Level Referral As Well. But The Person He Invites Becomes Your Second-Level Referral And You Will Receive 1% Comission From This Person’s Earnings. In Other Words, A Second-Level Referral Is A User Invited By A Person Whom You Invited To The Service.


Similarly, If Your Second-Level Referral Invites With His Own Referral Link A New User To The Service, Then This User Becomes Your Third-Level Referral. You Will Receive A 1% Comission From This Person’s Earnings.
